The Constitution acts as a guideline of basic human rights for the US government. It is controversial whether it applies to anyone on American soil, or just citizens. Yale Law Professor Christina Rodriguez believes that the Constitution includes everyone physically in the US when it uses “person” or “people” instead of “citizen”.
