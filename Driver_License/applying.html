"How do I apply for a driver's license, while undocumented?"

Applying for a California Driver's License

How to get a driver's licensewith the California DMV

  • Visit your local CA DMV office.
  • Complete driver's education and must obtain a learner's permit for at least 60 days (18-)
  • Complete a Driver License or Identification Card Application.
  • Provide proof of your identity, Social Security Number, and residency.
  • Pay the $35 driver's license fee.
  • Pass the written test.
  • Pass the road test.

Types of California Driver's License:

  • Class C
  • Noncommercial Class A
  • Noncommercial Class B
