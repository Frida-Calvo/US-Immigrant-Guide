The N-400 is the name of the application for naturalization, and it requires many specific documents to go along with it. There is also a $725 application fee. Click here to learn about fee waivers.

Depending on your situation you will most likely need to attach additional documents, but everyone filing an N-400 needs:

1. A photocopy of both sides of your Permanent Resident Card

2. A check for the application fee.

3. Two identical color photographs of you with your name and Alien Registration Number.
