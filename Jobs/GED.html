About Us

You have more job opportunities when you have a high school diploma or an equivalent certification.

The General Education Development (GED®) and the High School Equivalency Test (HiSet®) are two similar tests that help people who weren’t able to earn a high school diploma the traditional way. With one of these certifications, you are now able to take credit classes at CCSF and any other college. The City College of San Francisco offers preparation classes for both GED and HiSet tests FREE of charge.
