The City College of San Francisco (CCSF) offers tuition free non-credit college classes for anyone and all SF Residents now qualify for tuition free credit college classes. CCSF also offers free non-credit classes for everyone.

  • Free Non-Credit Classes

To do CCSF credit college classes you need to meet at least one requirement:

  • Be currently enrolled in a high school in SF
  • Have a high school diploma
